About

Factorygym is the first fitness gym in UK with a focus on functional training. We offer classes designed specifically for each individual’s goals, whether it be to improve their mobility, get stronger or burn fat. Our trainers are certified and our equipment is top-of-the-line so you can work out without worrying about your safety. Factorygym offers affordable monthly membership packages that include unlimited access to all of our workouts five days per week, two personal training sessions per month, one nutrition consultation session every three months and discounts on retail items in the locker room.
